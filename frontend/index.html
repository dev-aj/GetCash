We Are GetCash

Peer-to-Peer Lending is about individuals transacting between themselves for small personal loans, without the need to go through a financial intermediary such as a bank or any other financial institution. It is an exciting new way and a very efficient model that matches people who want to lend with people who want to borrow, giving everyone a better deal. GetCash is an online financial marketplace that matches lenders and borrowers in a safe and secure environment. GetCash simplify the process of lending and borrowing for their members.
